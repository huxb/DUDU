Frank Underwood

Francis J. "Frank" Underwood is a fictional character and the protagonist of the American version of House of Cards. Frank is described as manipulative, conniving, Machiavellian, sociopathic, and even evil. Throughout the series, he manipulates and destroys several people for his own ends, and commits murder twice.

Frank might be a hypocrite and a murderer, but I admire him for his ability to know what he wants and stop at nothing to achieve it. He never once deviated from his goal to obtain as much political power as possible, and that is admirable.
